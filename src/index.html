Welcome to Ontario Bird Sanctuary

Ontario Bird Sanctuary is the largest wild bird preservation site, located in outside of Toronto Ontario, Canada.
There are 123 species including 41 endangered species in the sanctuary.
OBS is non-profit organization. 32 volunteered workers take care of birds and environment here. Your donation will support our bird preservation. We will appreciate your support!
